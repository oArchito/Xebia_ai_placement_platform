## Project Summary

The AI-Powered Placement Management Platform was planned, analyzed, and documented as an individual project. All activities including stakeholder analysis, requirement gathering, feature identification, feature prioritization, product requirement documentation, system planning, architecture design, AI feature planning, wireframe creation, and report preparation were completed independently.

The project focuses on improving the campus placement process through centralized management, AI-assisted decision-making, automated candidate evaluation, intelligent job recommendations, and data-driven analytics. The proposed solution aims to enhance efficiency for students, recruiters, placement coordinators, and administrators while providing a scalable foundation for future development and advanced AI-powered capabilities.
